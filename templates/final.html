About

In this competitive mobile phone market we simply can't rely on assumptions. To estimate the price of mobile phones that a company creates, we have collected sales data of mobile phones of various companies. This data includes features of a mobile phone like RAM, Battery power etc. Using this data we are going to develop a Machine Learning model through which we will estimate the price range of a new mobile phone when the features are provided. Thus, we name our project as 'Fone Forecaster' - the model that forecasts the price of a mobile phone for the features specified.
